Who Are We

Greenviro Solutions Sdn Bhd (Greenviro) is a Bumiputera Status Company registered with the Ministry of Finance. Established in July 2014, having a corporate office located in Bandar Perda, Bukit Mertajam, Pulau Pinang.

Greenviro specializes in integrated waste management industries, has the strength, professionalism and strong financial backing. The company has the expertise, technical know-how and resources to provide reliable and effective integrated waste management (collection, public cleansing, transfer, recycling, and disposal). Besides integrated waste management services, the company is also involved in green and renewable energy such as solar systems, methane gas collection from landfills (gas to energy), etc.

Greenviro has been appointed by local authorities and private sectors to manage and operate waste management facilities (landfills, transfer stations, MRF, etc.) which are located locally and abroad such as in Penang, Pahang, Kelantan, Melaka, Kedah, Labuan and Brunei Darussalam.
